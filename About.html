YOUR JOURNEY TO FINANCIAL FREEDOM!

Khanyezi aims to empower communities to invest in their youths through decentralized finance

 

Our Mission

We believe that the current student loan system in South Africa excludes many students with potential, as they often require a guarantor to co-sign their student loan. Our mission is to empower the global community to invest in the youth through blockchain based financing. We do this by offering obligation-free student loans to post-graduate students and repackaging the loans into a tradable security for investors to invest in. This is done by tokenization.

Our Products and Services

We offer collateral-free loans to post-graduate students by creating a social investment product that allows investors to participate in funding their studies. These loans are packaged into an investible product that investors can purchase, through buying our KhanyeziToken.

Why Tokens

Tokens serve as a digital wrapper that give us the power to create, configure, and trade any imaginable asset frictionlessly, peer to peer. In short, tokenization can streamline our current financial markets, and allow us to create a world filled with seamless value transfer.
